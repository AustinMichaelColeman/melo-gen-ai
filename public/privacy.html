MeloGenAI

A playlist generator plugin for ChatGPT and for YouTube Music™

MeloGenAI Plugin Privacy Policy

Updated May 30, 2023

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes the type of personal information we collect from users ("Users", "you", "your") of our MeloGenAI plugin (the "Plugin") for ChatGPT and for YouTube Music™, how we use, share and protect this information. This API Client uses YouTube API Services. See the Google Privacy Policy. This is a plugin for ChatGPT, created by OpenAI. See the OpenAI privacy policy. This plugin is hosted using Vercel. See the Vercel privacy policy.

2. Information Collection

When using the Plugin, we collect the following information:

  • Your YouTube account details for the purposes of generating playlists for YouTube Music™
  • The title submitted to ChatGPT for the playlist being generated
  • The names of songs you want to query YouTube for playlist generation
  • The privacy setting you choose for your playlist. If none is specified, it will default to public.

We don't collect any information beyond what's necessary for the Plugin to function.

3. Information Use

We use your information only for the context of the Plugin's functionality and not for any other purposes.

4. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data collected through the Plugin. All data is used solely for the purpose of generating the playlist and is not stored. While we do not store any data, the third-party services (YouTube, OpenAI, and Vercel) may have their own data retention policies.

5. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. These may include the right to access your data, the right to correct inaccurate data, and the right to request deletion of your data. As we do not retain any personal data, these rights may not be applicable.

You can revoke the Plugin's access to your data via the Google security settings page.

6. Public Playlists

Please note that the playlists generated by the Plugin will be public by default and associated with your YouTube Music™ account. You can request the plugin generate the playlist either as public, private, or unlisted. The plugin instructs ChatGPT to provide you with the list of songs we plan to add to the playlist and the name of the playlist prior to creation. The plugin is instructed to ask your consent prior to actually generating the playlist.

7. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties, unless it's necessary for the Plugin's functionality or if we are legally required to do so.

8. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, we cannot guarantee absolute security as no method of online transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Plugin is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has interacted with our Plugin, we will inform the relevant parties to cease the use of such information.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, so please review it periodically.

11. Contact Us
